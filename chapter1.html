THE ENCHANTED GARDEN

The garden Iona glowed with quiet magic, like it had been waiting just for them. Iona gently touched a soft, flower.

"It feels alive," she whispered. Amaia touched the shiny grass and saw little glowing footprints appear. "It’s like the garden knows we’re here," she said, smiling.

In front of them, two paths appeared: To the left, a glowing butterfly flew through the silver mist. Its wings left little sparkles in the air.

To the right, a hedgehog peeked out from under twisty vines. Its tiny eyes watched them closely.

Amaia looked at both paths. "Should we follow the light or go where it’s quiet?"

It was their choice.
